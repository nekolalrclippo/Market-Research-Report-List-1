<p><p>Silicon wafer etching acids are essential in semiconductor manufacturing, used to remove unwanted materials and define microstructures. Hydrofluoric acid is primarily used for silicon dioxide removal due to its effectiveness in etching glass and ceramics. Nitric acid serves to clean and oxidize silicon surfaces, enhancing adherence during further processing. Mixed acids, often combining hydrofluoric and nitric acids, provide versatile etching solutions, allowing precise control over the etching process for various applications, including integrated circuits and solar cells.</p></p>
