<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Dog Vaccines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Attenuated Live Vaccines</li><li>Conjugate Vaccines</li><li>Inactivated Vaccines</li><li>Subunit Vaccines</li><li>Toxoid Vaccines</li><li>DNA Vaccines</li><li>Recombinant Vaccines</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dog vaccines market comprises various types, each serving specific purposes. Attenuated live vaccines use weakened pathogens to build immunity. Conjugate vaccines link polysaccharides to proteins, enhancing immune response. Inactivated vaccines contain killed pathogens, providing safety without risk of disease. Subunit vaccines consist of pieces of the virus or bacteria, focusing immune response effectively. Toxoid vaccines target inactivated toxins from pathogens. DNA vaccines introduce genetic material to produce an immune reaction, while recombinant vaccines utilize genetically engineered organisms to create protective antigens.</p></p>
