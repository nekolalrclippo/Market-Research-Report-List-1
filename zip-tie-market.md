<p><p>The zip tie market can be categorized into single loop and double loop zip ties. Single loop zip ties feature a single fastening point, ideal for securing cables and items quickly and efficiently. They are widely used in various industries for bundling and organizing. In contrast, double loop zip ties have a secondary loop that allows for more versatile fastening options, making them suitable for heavier-duty applications. Both types offer durability, versatility, and ease of use, catering to different fastening needs.</p></p>
