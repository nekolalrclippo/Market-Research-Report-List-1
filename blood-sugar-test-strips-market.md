<p><p>The blood sugar test strips market can be categorized into three main types: glucose oxidase, glucose dehydrogenase, and others. Glucose oxidase strips utilize an enzyme that reacts with glucose, generating a measurable electrical current. Glucose dehydrogenase strips employ an alternative enzyme, allowing for a broader range of glucose measurement, including in varied oxygen levels. Other types may include innovative technologies or multi-parameter strips that provide additional health metrics, expanding the options available for blood glucose monitoring.</p></p>
