<p><p>The VOC's rotor market encompasses two primary types: zeolite VOC's rotors and activated carbon VOC's rotors. Zeolite rotors utilize synthetic or natural zeolite materials that efficiently adsorb volatile organic compounds due to their porous structure, providing a high surface area for trapping pollutants. On the other hand, activated carbon rotors employ activated carbon, renowned for its exceptional adsorption capabilities, to capture a wide range of VOCs. Both types are essential in industrial air purification systems, improving air quality and compliance with environmental regulations.</p></p>
