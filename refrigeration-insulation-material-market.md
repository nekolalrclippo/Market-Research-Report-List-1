<p><p>The refrigeration insulation materials market encompasses various types, each serving specific thermal insulation needs. Elastomeric foam is flexible and moisture-resistant, ideal for HVAC systems. Polyurethane (PU) and polyisocyanurate (PIR) foam offer high insulation value and efficiency. Polystyrene foam, both expanded (EPS) and extruded (XPS), is lightweight and durable. Fiberglass is widely used for its non-combustible properties and versatility. Phenolic foam provides excellent thermal performance and fire resistance. Other materials may include cellulose and mineral wool, catering to diverse applications.</p></p>
