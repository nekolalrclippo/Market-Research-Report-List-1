<p><p>The HVAC adhesives and sealants market includes various types, notably hybrid adhesives and neutral cure silicone sealants. Hybrid adhesives combine the properties of different adhesive technologies, offering versatility and strong bonding for HVAC applications. Neutral cure silicone sealants, on the other hand, cure without releasing acetic acid, making them ideal for use in sensitive environments where corrosion could be a concern. Both types are essential for ensuring efficient sealing, preventing air leaks, and maintaining system performance in HVAC installations.</p></p>
