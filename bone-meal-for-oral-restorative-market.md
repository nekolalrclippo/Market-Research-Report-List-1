<p><p>Bone meal for oral restorative applications primarily includes autogenous tooth powder and xenogeneic beef bone meal. Autogenous tooth powder is derived from the patient's own dental tissue, promoting biocompatibility and minimizing rejection risks. In contrast, xenogeneic beef bone meal comes from bovine sources and is treated to ensure safety and sterility, offering a viable option for bone grafting and enhancing dental procedures. Both types serve to promote healing and integration in oral reconstructive treatments.</p></p>
